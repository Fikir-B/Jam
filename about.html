About Jam

Jam is your free traffic-avoidance tool for Addis Ababa. We help you see streets, avoid congested areas, and reach your destination faster. All completely free, powered by OpenStreetMap and Leaflet.js.

Our goal is to make commuting easier, smarter, and more fun.
